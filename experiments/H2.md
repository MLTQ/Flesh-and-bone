# H2 — nonuniform morphogenesis and wound repair

## Question

Can a continuous tissue-demand field allocate freely fed cells into anatomy that
is not a uniform bone offset, then recruit fresh cells to a wound, restore the
correct material field, mature those cells locally, and survive articulation?

## Predeclared morphology

- Tapered thick upper-left limb with a terminal bulb.
- Thin bridge and thin upper-right limb.
- Widening lower-right limb.
- Off-axis pad at the right junction.
- The original two surrounded negative spaces remain required.
- The wound removes the upper terminal-bulb patch.

## Arms

1. Hierarchical live tissue deficit + spatial target density + pressure +
   plastic material. A coarse region guide is allocated from unfilled regional
   capacity and stored hysteretically; continuous within-region attention
   supplies position. No target site or final coordinate is supplied.
2. Nearest bone + uniform density pressure.
3. Live deficit with pressure disabled.
4. Live deficit with first-contact material identity.

No arm receives or stores a target-site assignment. The learned neural residual
remains zero so H2 measures the developmental substrate.

## Protocol

1. Feed one target mass of generation-0 cells from below and assemble at rest.
2. Lock the mature initial tissue.
3. Delete cells occupying the predeclared terminal-bulb wound.
4. Feed exactly the removed mass as generation-1 undifferentiated cells.
5. Permit generation-1 cells to mature only after sustained low velocity,
   near-target density, and proximity to valid tissue.
6. After local lock, use the acquired attachment as an invariant with no live
   deficit blend.
7. Begin skeleton motion only after the repair window.

## Acceptance criteria

- Pre-wound total coverage at least 0.85 and every anatomical region at least
  0.70 covered.
- Damage lowers wound coverage by at least 0.30.
- Repair restores wound coverage to at least 0.78 and to within 0.12 of its
  pre-wound value.
- Healthy-region coverage loses no more than 0.08 during repair.
- At least 0.55 of generation-1 cells localize to the wound region.
- At least 0.90 of generation-1 cells commit and locally lock before motion.
- Repaired and moving checker-field accuracy at least 0.80.
- Region allocation total-variation error no more than 0.16.
- Final moving coverage at least 0.82, mean tracking error at most 0.12,
  crowding below 0.08, and both negative-space occupancies at most 0.02.
- All target assignments remain hidden (`assigned_site == -1`), mass is
  accounted for, and all state remains finite.

## Results

### Frozen primary configuration

The final configuration uses a seven-way persistent region guide, broad
within-region attention while a cell is remote, and local deficit attention
after arrival. Density pressure remains active throughout assembly and repair.
The initial generation is locked before damage; generation-1 cells mature only
after 18 consecutive stable local observations. Locked cells have no live
deficit blend during motion. The deficit log weight is 2.2.

All three validation seeds pass every criterion:

|---:|---:|---:|---:|---:|---:|---:|---:|---:|---:|---:|:---:|
| 7 | 0.914 | 0.886 | 0.966 | 0.069 | 0.897 | 0.931 | 1.000 | 0.888 | 0.878 | 0.888 | yes |
| 19 | 0.898 | 0.860 | 0.897 | 0.103 | 0.966 | 1.000 | 1.000 | 0.865 | 0.882 | 0.852 | yes |
| 31 | 0.922 | 0.886 | 0.966 | 0.138 | 0.897 | 1.000 | 0.964 | 0.927 | 0.882 | 0.870 | yes |

Seed 7's repaired healthy coverage is 0.917, region-allocation error is 0.016,
moving tracking error is 0.070, crowding is 0.00013, and both negative-space
occupancies are zero. The corresponding run has 245 active cells, 245 coarse
region guides, and zero exposed site assignments. All state is finite and mass
is exactly accounted for.

The pressure-off localization score is not evidence of repair: nearly all
tissue is collapsed into a small area, so the regional label is trivially
concentrated while wound coverage remains 0.034. This is why repair coverage,
density, maturation, and motion gates are evaluated together.

### Iteration record

The unsuccessful variants are part of the result:

1. **Strictly local H1 field (`h2_initial`).** Pre-wound coverage reached only
   0.702 and no fresh cell localized to the wound. The lower feed source was
   captured by closer healthy deficits before it could reach the remote bulb.
2. **Recomputed hard region choice (`h2_hierarchical`).** Allowing fate to be
   reselected every step caused guide chatter and scattered tissue; pre-wound
   coverage fell to 0.437.
3. **Persistent guide with broad regional attraction (`h2_hysteretic`).** This
   repaired the wound from 0.034 to 0.862 with 0.968 localization, but cells
   centroid-clumped inside regions and pre-wound coverage remained 0.767.
4. **Distance-adaptive attention (`h2_adaptive`).** Broad attraction is used
   only while remote; after arrival the query radius contracts to the H1 local
   scale. This restored connected regional tissue and passed seed 7. Seed 31
   narrowly missed the relative wound-recovery gate.
5. **Frozen H2 (`h2_final`).** Removing the locked deficit blend and raising the
   deficit log weight from 1.8 to 2.2 made the repair robust across all three
   seeds without changing the predeclared gates.

### Interpretation

H2 supports the proposed *fate first, local placement second* decomposition.
A stem-like cell need not know its final coordinate: a persistent coarse region
is enough to cross an otherwise fatal spatial credit-assignment gap, after which
local tissue deficit and pressure can place it. The wound experiment also shows
lineage-specific recruitment: 93.1–100% of the newly fed generation reaches the
removed bulb patch, while intact coverage is retained.

It does **not** prove pure local self-assembly. The guide has seven anatomical
values and reads global unfilled regional capacity. It is deliberately weaker
than H0's 245 unique target identities—every `assigned_site` remains `-1`—but it
is still privileged developmental information. Nor does H2 prove a neural
cellular automaton: the learned acceleration residual is identically zero. The
next experiment should learn the guide/fate decision and particle residuals
while retaining this controller as an upper bound and curriculum teacher.
