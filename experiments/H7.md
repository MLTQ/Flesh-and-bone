# H7 — bounded hybrid density mechanics

## Question

Can the structure-preserving H6C constitutive rule be extended with one useful
nonlinear tissue behavior without recovering the unstable extrapolation of the
H5/H6M unconstrained MLP? H7 adds only LBS-relative local compression pressure
and weaker stretch cohesion. It does not attempt contact, volume preservation,
muscle activation, or biological tissue identification.

## Frozen diagnostic and teacher

Before implementing H7, axial edge strain was measured on the existing linear
teachers. Replay had median/95th-percentile absolute strain of `0.020/0.155`;
the failed `1.526x` forcing had `0.042/0.329`. These measurements fixed the
nonlinear onset and caps before an H7 teacher or model was run.

For each directed six-neighbor edge, the equilibrium direction and length come
from the current LBS configuration. Residual displacement projected along that
direction gives a clipped dimensionless strain. Compression activates beyond
5%, stretch beyond 8%. The teacher averages squared excess strain vectors per
cell, applies stronger pressure in soft/far-from-bone tissue and weaker stretch
cohesion, then smoothly caps the combined density acceleration at `12 m/s²`.
The pressure coefficient varies from 30 to 50 and cohesion from 6 to 12.

This is a synthetic, graph- and LBS-conditioned curriculum. It is useful as a
controlled nonlinear mechanics test, not evidence that those constants describe
human flesh.

## Learned hybrid

The H6C five-term backbone is frozen. A `5 -> 32 -> 32 -> 2` MLP sees only
dimensionless scalar invariants: signed mean compression, compression RMS,
stretch RMS, softness, and local residual speed normalized by pitch and frame
rate. Sigmoid outputs are bounded to nonnegative pressure `[0, 60]` and cohesion
`[0, 18]`. Those scalars multiply the explicit equivariant pressure/cohesion
vectors and the result receives the same `12 m/s²` smooth norm cap.

The learned component therefore cannot invent a spatial direction, reverse a
restoring force with a negative coefficient, or create an unbounded density
acceleration. It can only modulate two declared local mechanisms.

## Split and controls

- Training motions: replay, reverse, and walk-then-hold.
- Qualification: half-speed, seed 7 only. This is the only permitted tuning
  surface; optimizer exposure or hidden width may be changed if the first run
  fails, while the teacher, features, bounds, splits, and gates remain fixed.
- Final holdouts after qualification: `1.526x` fast motion and the prepared
  Kimodo weight-shift cycle, seeds 7, 19, and 31.
- Controls: nonlinear teacher, frozen H6C backbone only, density-blind hybrid
  (identical to the backbone), and the historical unconstrained H5U rules from
  H6M as contextual evidence.

Training uses 300,000 deterministically sampled teacher states per motion,
1,200 optimizer steps, batch size 65,536, and a fixed 32-unit hidden width.
Every rollout lasts 20 cycles.

## Predeclared gates

Teacher and fit:

- finite state and cycle seam no greater than `0.5 mm`;
- density acceleration RMS in `[0.02, 4.0] m/s²` and maximum no greater than
  `12.0001 m/s²`;
- teacher residual RMS in `[1, 75] mm`;
- training density-acceleration NRMSE no greater than `0.10`;
- half-speed seed-7 density-acceleration NRMSE no greater than `0.15`.

Twenty-cycle rollout:

- position RMS no greater than `4 mm`, p99 no greater than `12 mm`, maximum no
  greater than `40 mm`, and cycle drift no greater than `3 mm`;
- secondary-motion amplitude ratio in `[0.75, 1.25]` and far/near amplitude
  ratio at least `1.25`;
- finite state and predicted density acceleration no greater than `12.0001
  m/s²`;
- the backbone-only position RMS must be at least `0.2 mm`, so the nonlinear
  task is non-vacuous;
- the hybrid must reduce backbone position RMS by at least 60% and sampled
  compression error by at least 50%.

Half-speed seed 7 must pass before final holdouts are examined. After that,
every seed must pass both fast and Kimodo holdouts for an aggregate H7 pass.

## Status

Initial qualification **failed as non-vacuous evidence**. The half-speed teacher
density force was `0.01437 m/s²` RMS and the backbone position error only
`0.0327 mm`, below the frozen `0.02 m/s²` and `0.2 mm` floors. Every other gate
passed: unseen one-step NRMSE was `0.00444`, 20-cycle hybrid RMS was `0.000111
mm`, and it removed 99.66% of backbone position error. Final holdouts were not
opened. H7B preserves the failure and predeclares a scale-only correction in
[`H7B.md`](H7B.md).
